Events

We host events all over the country! The biggest of these events is Subiefest.

Local events are hosted on the second saturday of each month. Monthly local meets take place in Orange, San Diego and L.A. counties. For more information check out our Join page above

SubieFest

Subiefest is the largest All-Subaru meet in North America. It consists of all Subarus regardless of model or year. Sponsored by a mulitude of vendors, there will be a assortment of aftermarket modications for all subarus! Come join us October 10th, 2015 at the AAA Speedway in Fontana, CA. There will be food, merchandise, racing, music and a raffle. Each vendor will raffle off some amazing prizes! Tickets are $15 at the gate. Gates open at 10am.





Subiefest Competitions

Exhaust Competition
Make them loud! Our exhaust competition is a face between the loudest Subarus around. Whether straight-pipe or custom exhaust, all are welcome. A great prize will be given to the winner!

Auto-X
Subarus will make their way through a gut-wrenching course full of twists and turns. This tests handling and acceleration. Each racer will be timed and the racer with the fastest time will walk away victorious.

Track
Racers will compete on a oval Nascar style track. This race will consist of five laps and only one attempt. Simple set of rules, each racer is given on chance to complete the five fastest laps of the day. Winner of this competition will recieve the ultimate prize of Subiefest!

*Note:Each racer must have a race helmet that meets the uniform standards required by the Department of Motor Vehicles
