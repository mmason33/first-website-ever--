The Subaru Fan Club is group passionate people dedicated to building, driving and racing Subarus! Everyone is welcome regardless of experience.

Our Team

Beginner
First time subaru owner, has not modified a Subaru, has never joined a car club and has never raced on a track.

Amateur
Possibly has owned mutiple Subarus. Has modified or knows the steps to modify a Subaru. May have spent some time in the car club world. Usually have little or no experience on the race track.

Advanced
These are the few that have dedicated a lot of money and time to their passion. Usually long time Subaru owners, advanced modifications on their cars and are sometimes classified as amateur track professionals. Putting hours and money in countless days on the track. The leaders of our club reside in this class.

Our Mission

  • To create and maintain a venue for all Subaru enthusiasts
  • To promote racing in a safe controlled environment
  • Most of all enjoy our passion alongside family, friends and fellow racers!
